Beck A

My roots are in California and my family originates within the Pueblo Nation borders in New Mexico. During the mid 1800's my maternal grandfather's great grandparents immigrated from Basque country where their migration trail was through the northern Atlantic, crossing the Canadian border and down the middle of the country. My maternal grandmother was raised in Santa Fe and Alberquerque, New Mexico just outside the Tiwa Reservation (this would be her tribe). Recently during a visit with the elders and at their table, they told me that they were not allowed to say they were indigenous because the white people were abducting or disappering first nation people. Today doesnt seem much different.
